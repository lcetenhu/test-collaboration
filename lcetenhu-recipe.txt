1. Chop 3 cloves of garlic
2. Chop 1 chilli pepper
3. Put them to a heat-resistant bowl
4. Add 500 g of cherry tomatoes
5. Add 500 g of feta cheese
6. Give them an olive oil bath (don't hesitate, use a lot of oil)
7. Put the bowl to oven at 220 degrees and let it bake for ~30 min
8. Boil water
9. Add salt to the boiling water
10. Add pasta and cook until al dente
11. Add pasta to the baked tomato-feta bowl and mix
